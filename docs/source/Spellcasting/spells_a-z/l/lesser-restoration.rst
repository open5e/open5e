Lesser Restoration
------------------

2nd-level abjuration
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

**Casting Time:** 1 action

**Range:** Touch

**Components:** V, S

**Duration:** Instantaneous

You touch a creature and can end either one disease or one condition
afflicting it. The condition can be blinded, deafened, paralyzed, or
poisoned.
